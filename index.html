Frequently Asked Questions

How much does window cleaning cost?

Our residential window cleaning starts from £15 for a 2-bedroom house. Prices depend on property size and frequency of service.

Do I need to be home during cleaning?

No, you don't need to be home for exterior cleaning. For interior windows, we'll arrange convenient access.

How often should windows be cleaned?

We recommend every 4-8 weeks for residential properties to maintain clarity and prevent damage.

Are you insured?

Yes, we carry full public liability insurance and all our team members are trained professionals.
