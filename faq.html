How much does window cleaning cost?

Residential window cleaning starts from £15 for a 2-bedroom house. Prices vary by property size and frequency.

Do I need to be home during cleaning?

No. Exterior cleaning does not require you to be at home. For interior windows we’ll arrange a convenient time.

How often should windows be cleaned?

We recommend every 4–8 weeks to maintain clarity and prevent damage.

Are you insured?

Yes, we carry full public liability insurance and all team members are trained professionals.
